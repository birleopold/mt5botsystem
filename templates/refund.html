Refund Policy

Our Commitment

Refunds are issued at our discretion. We strive to ensure all customers are satisfied with our services, but understand that sometimes refunds may be necessary.

Refund Process

  1. Contact our support team with your payment details
  2. Provide a clear reason for requesting the refund
  3. Our team will review your request within 48 hours
  4. If approved, refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days

Eligibility Criteria

  • Requests must be made within 14 days of purchase
  • Service quality issues must be documented
  • Unused or partially used services may be eligible for partial refunds

Please contact support with your payment details and reason for requesting a refund. We will review your request and respond promptly.
