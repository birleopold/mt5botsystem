Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our platform

Creating an account is simple:

  1. Click on the "Register" button in the top navigation
  2. Fill in your details in the registration form
  3. Verify your email address by clicking the link sent to your inbox
  4. Log in with your new credentials

Once registered, you'll have access to your dashboard and can explore our platform features.

We offer multiple payment options to accommodate our global user base:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: Process securely through Stripe
  • Cryptocurrency: Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other major coins via Coinbase and Binance
  • Manual/Cash Payments: Available in select regions (contact support for details)
  • Bank Transfer: Available for annual subscriptions

All transactions are secured with industry-standard encryption protocols.

Subscribing to our service is straightforward:

  1. Navigate to the Plans page
  2. Browse available subscription options
  3. Select your preferred plan
  4. Complete the checkout process
  5. Gain immediate access to premium features

We offer monthly, quarterly, and annual billing cycles with discounts for longer commitments.

We offer multiple support channels to assist you:

  • Contact Form: Use our Contact page to send a direct message
  • Support Tickets: Submit a detailed ticket through the ticket system
  • Live Chat: Available during business hours for immediate assistance
  • Email: Reach us at support@theautomata.com

Our support team is available 24/7 and typically responds within 1-2 business hours.

Managing your subscription is easy:

  • Change Plan: Log into your account, navigate to Subscription settings, and select "Change Plan"
  • Cancel Subscription: Go to Subscription settings and click "Cancel Subscription"
  • Assistance: If you need help, contact our support team for immediate assistance

Please note that refunds are processed according to our Refund Policy.

Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing cycle unless otherwise specified.

Setting up your trading bot involves a few simple steps:

  1. Navigate to the Bots section in your dashboard
  2. Click "Create New Bot" and select your preferred strategy
  3. Configure your risk parameters and trading preferences
  4. Connect your exchange API (with trading permissions)
  5. Activate your bot and monitor its performance

For detailed setup instructions, please refer to our Bot Setup Guide.

Our legal documents are easily accessible:

  • Our Privacy Policy details how we collect, use, and protect your data
  • The Terms of Service outlines the rules and guidelines for using our platform

Links to these documents are also available in the footer of every page on our website.
